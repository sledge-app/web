View settings

View settings.

To see the view parameters, select first a view in the side menu. Then, on the right, click on the settings icon, as shown in the given screenshot.

You can setup a background for the whole view. It can be an image, a color, or a pattern. The background will cover the page background, but will be covered by the components contents and backgrounds.
